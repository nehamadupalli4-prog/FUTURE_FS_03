# Royal Family Restaurant Website

## Project Overview

This project was developed as part of **Future Interns – Full Stack Web Development Task 3 (2026)**.

The objective was to build a professional website for a real local business and present it as a client-ready solution that improves online visibility, customer trust, and business growth.

The selected business is **Royal Family Restaurant**, a family-friendly restaurant offering quality dining experiences and a diverse menu.

---

## Problem Statement

Many local restaurants rely primarily on walk-in customers and word-of-mouth marketing. Without a professional online presence, potential customers may struggle to:

* Find restaurant information
* View menus and services
* Make reservations
* Contact the business easily
* Build trust before visiting

---

## Solution

A modern, responsive restaurant website was designed and developed to help Royal Family Restaurant:

* Showcase its menu and services
* Increase customer engagement
* Improve brand credibility
* Enable easy reservations
* Provide quick contact options
* Create a stronger online presence
